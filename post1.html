7 Skincare Mistakes Ruining Your Skin

Even if you're following a skincare routine, small mistakes can ruin your results.

1. Overwashing Your Face

Washing too often strips natural oils and damages your skin barrier.

2. Skipping Sunscreen

UV damage causes aging, dark spots, and acne scars.

3. Using Too Many Products

More is not better. Keep your routine simple.
